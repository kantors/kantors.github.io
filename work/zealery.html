About Zealery

Zealery is a social shopping app designed to give users the experience of shopping with friends. Users can see clothing recommendations, browse by style and view what clothing their friends have liked. It was important to our clients that the app was a fun way users could get their friends opinions while shopping online. As a result, there is a Runway feature where users can ask their friends to vote on different pieces of clothing.
