Everyday Boston

Scout Labs is a multidisciplinary team of students who partner with local organizations as design consultants for social good. We partnered with Everyday Boston, a community building organization that uses storytelling and journalism to connect people in Boston. We worked closely with our client to define the role that Everyday Boston's website plays in the organization's larger goal of promoting curiosity and connection in Boston. Our final deliverable is the design and development of a new Everyday Boston website.
