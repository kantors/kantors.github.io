You might not know it,
but your data is being mined for profit.

Every click, every search and every website you visit generates a small amount of data. While this data might seem insignificant to you, all of your online actions are tracked to create a comprehensive profile of your Internet habits. The reason that this happens is very simple. The data you create is incredibly valuable to advertisers.

Frighteningly, your data does not belong to you. You give it away as a result of using most of the popular online services from Facebook to Google and YouTube. In many ways, this data generation is the reason why these services are free. For example Facebook is much more than a social networking site.
