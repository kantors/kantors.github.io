Some apps make it difficult to log out by forcing the user take extra steps to find the log out button. This is a simple solution that helps minimize the amount of information websites can collect about you.

Logout of Facebook on IPhone:

  • 1. Open the Facebook app
  • 2. Open the menu
  • 3. Select More
  • 4. Select Logout button
  • 5. Confirm Logout

Logout of Instagram on IPhone:

  • 1. Open Instagram app
  • 2. Tap the silhouette icon
  • 3. Tap the Settings menu
  • 4. Tap Logout
  • 5. Confirm Logout
