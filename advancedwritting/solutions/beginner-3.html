Delete Cookies

Cookies are not just for eating! The cookies that exist on your computer are actually small files of data collected by a specific website. They make websites load faster but they also allow websites to collect information about what you do online.

Delete your cookies in Chrome:

  • 1. Open your browser toolbar and selecting more
  • 2. Click the history
  • 3. Click clear browsing data
  • 4. Under clear browsing data check cookies
  • 5. Click clear browsing data

Delete your cookies in Safari:

  • 1. Select preferences from the Safari menu
  • 2. Select the privacy tab
  • 3. Click remove all website data
